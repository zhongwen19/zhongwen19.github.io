About

I am a PhD student in MRI at the School of Biomedical Engineering, Tsinghua University. My research focuses on MRI sequence development, real-time image reconstruction, and diffusion MRI, with an emphasis on methods that are technically rigorous and practical for advanced imaging studies.

I am advised by Prof. Hua Guo . My current interests include sequence development on Siemens platforms, advanced diffusion encoding, high-resolution diffusion imaging, and real-time MRI methods for interventional applications.

Sequences / Tools

The following Siemens IDEA platform sequences are available upon application or academic collaboration.

  1. External Diffusion Gradient wavEform

    Supports arbitrary external diffusion waveforms for applications such as QTI, motion compensation, and OGSE. Platform: VE11E.

  2. OGSE / PGSE C2P

    Distributed through the C2P platform and used by more than 20 institutions. Available on VE11E, VE11C, XA20, XA30, XA50, XA60, and XA61. Dedicated versions have also been developed for CimaX and Connectom 2.0. Multiple publications have already been produced using this sequence at external sites.

  3. Interleaved EPI

    An interleaved EPI sequence available on multiple Siemens software platforms, including VE11E, VE11C, XA20, and XA30.

  4. SMSlab

    A high-resolution, high-SNR 3D DWI sequence. Platform: VE11E and VE12U. [Related paper]

  5. ptx 3D DWI

    A pTx-enabled 3D DWI sequence. Platform: VE12U.

  6. PSF DWI C2P

    A high-resolution distortion-free 2D DWI sequence available through C2P. Platform: VE11E and XA30.

Projects

  1. Comparative Study of PSF and TGSE-BLADE Sequences for Diffusion-Weighted Imaging of Skull Base Tumors

    Sep. 2022 - Jun. 2023

    Developed and optimized a PSF DWI sequence on Siemens Prisma 3T and carried out a prospective patient study comparing PSF DWI and TGSE-BLADE DWI for skull base tumor imaging.

    [Paper] [Abstract] [Poster]

  2. Development and Evaluation of a Second-Order Motion-Compensated 3D Spiral Acquisition for Robust Whole-Brain DWI

    May 2024 - Nov. 2024

    Developed a second-order motion-compensated 3D spiral DWI sequence using Pulseq and investigated its feasibility for high-quality whole-brain DWI without cardiac gating, including evaluation of SNR and SNR efficiency against other DWI methods.

    [Paper] [Abstract] [Manuscript]

  3. Investigation and Development of OGSE Sequence for Microstructure Imaging on the Connectom 2.0 High-Performance 3T Platform

    Jun. 2024 - Nov. 2024

    Optimized OGSE timing and protocol for the Connectom 2.0 system, acquired high-quality phantom and in-vivo data, and focused on efficient diffusion microstructure imaging.

    [Paper] [Abstract] [Poster]

  4. Investigation and Development of Low-Field MRI-Guided Real-Time Imaging in Cardiac Ablation Intervention Surgery Ongoing

    Dec. 2024 - Present

    Ongoing work on low-field MRI-guided real-time imaging for cardiac intervention, including reconstruction and system optimization for faster and clearer guidance imaging.

    [Abstract] [Poster] [Manuscript]
